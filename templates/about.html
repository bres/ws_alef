Aleph is the Phoenician letter that led to the first letter of the greek alphabet “Alpha”.
As a word it symbolizes the beginning of the creation as well as the starting point of human culture.
Our primary focus is on the creation of handmade bags and accesories, using several high quality eco friendly and materials with different textures.
Our philosophy is based on finding the roots of human creativity in products made purely by hand.
We are inspired by the past to create unique and timeless products.

Handmade

Our products are handmade and therefore, unique.
That makes ALEF collection literally limited edition.
Our bags are made exclusively in ALEF atelier that’s based in Athens.
We make our bags with love and joy so you can have a unique product.

Materials

The collection of fabrics is based on sustainability and durability ensuring they are of the highest possible quality and produced in an environmentally friendly way. Our top priority is to create the best product possible and at the same time be part of the solution for a sustainable green planet.

Behind the brand

ALEF brand was born in July of 2020, during a difficult season of humanity, from Krisana Alefantinou.
We felt that through the insecurity and fear that was all around us at the time, we needed to place our focus in regeneration and consequently in creation.
Krisana studied Industrial Design and Theatre in Greece.
Those two paths and her insurmountable need to create, led her to design women’s bags with minimal design

Our world needs more caring.
With that in mind, we choose to donate every year, in different foundation, 2% of our online sales. That way, we can spread the love that you so openly give us.
For 2022, we chose to donate to ANIMA organization.
ANIMA is a foundation that cares and protects the wild life that resides in urban environments and often get injured. Anima count exclusively on donations and subscribers. Having seen up close their efforts and from our deep appreciation of all people that consist Anima, we would like to contribute in the way we can.
Every life is important and we can help to find its path.
Meet ANIMA through their official page wild-anima.gr
