About

Aleph is the Phoenician letter that led to the first letter of the greek alphabet “Alpha”.
As a word it symbolizes the beginning of the creation as well as the starting point of human culture.
Our primary focus is on the creation of handmade bags and accesories, using several high quality eco friendly and materials with different textures.
Our philosophy is based on finding the roots of human creativity in products made purely by hand.
We are inspired by the past to create unique and timeless products.

Materials

The collection of fabrics is based on sustainability and durability.
Our leather bags are made from eco-friendly and/or recycled leather.
That way we can ensure the best results for our products and less damage for the environment.

Handmade

Our products are handmade and therefore, unique.
That makes ALEF collection literally limited edition.
Our bags are made exclusively in ALEF atelier that’s based in Athens.
We make our bags with love and joy so you can have a unique product.
